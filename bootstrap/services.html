Our Services

Lawn Care

At Lance Co-operative, we understand that a lush, green lawn is the pride of any homeowner. Our comprehensive lawn care services include meticulous mowing, precise fertilization schedules tailored to your specific grass type, and effective pest control solutions that ensure your lawn remains healthy and vibrant throughout the seasons. We also offer seasonal clean-up services to prepare your lawn for winter and rejuvenate it in the spring, ensuring that it looks its best year-round.

Landscape Design

Our landscape design services are crafted to transform your outdoor space into a stunning oasis that reflects your personal style and enhances the beauty of your property. Our team of experienced designers works closely with you to understand your vision, preferences, and budget. We incorporate a variety of elements such as plants, trees, shrubs, pathways, and decorative features to create a harmonious landscape that not only meets your aesthetic desires but also promotes sustainability and environmental health.

Hardscaping

Hardscaping is an essential aspect of landscaping that involves the use of non-plant elements to enhance your outdoor space. Our hardscaping services include the installation of patios, walkways, retaining walls, and decorative stone features that add functionality and visual appeal to your property. We use high-quality materials and expert craftsmanship to ensure durability and longevity in all our hardscaping projects. Whether you're looking to create an inviting outdoor entertaining area or simply improve accessibility around your home, we have the expertise to bring your vision to life.

Irrigation Systems

Efficient irrigation is crucial for maintaining a healthy landscape while conserving water resources. Our team specializes in designing and installing customized irrigation systems that cater specifically to the needs of your garden or lawn. We utilize advanced technology such as drip irrigation and smart controllers that adjust watering schedules based on weather conditions, ensuring that every plant receives the right amount of water without waste. With our irrigation solutions, you can enjoy a thriving landscape with minimal effort.
